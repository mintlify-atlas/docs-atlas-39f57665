<div className="mt-16 mb-16 max-w-5xl mx-auto px-6">
  <h2 className="text-3xl font-bold text-gray-900 dark:text-white mb-4 text-center">Quick Start</h2>
  <p className="text-base text-gray-600 dark:text-gray-400 mb-8 text-center max-w-2xl mx-auto">
    Get WeakAuras running in your WotLK client in minutes
  </p>

  <Steps>
    <Step title="Download WeakAuras">
      Download the latest release from GitHub and extract all folders to your `Interface\AddOns` directory.
      
      ```bash
      # Extract to your WoW directory
      World of Warcraft/Interface/AddOns/WeakAuras
      World of Warcraft/Interface/AddOns/WeakAurasOptions
      World of Warcraft/Interface/AddOns/WeakAurasTemplates
      ```
      
      <Note>Make sure you extract all addon folders from the archive to ensure full functionality.</Note>
    </Step>

    <Step title="Open the Configuration">
      Launch World of Warcraft and type `/wa` or `/weakauras` in chat to open the configuration interface.
      
      You can also access it through:
      - The minimap icon
      - Interface options menu
    </Step>

    <Step title="Create Your First Aura">
      Click the "New" button in the WeakAuras interface and select a display type. Configure your trigger conditions to show when specific buffs, debuffs, or game events occur.
      
      <Tip>Start with the Icon display type for tracking buffs and debuffs — it's the most commonly used display type.</Tip>
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
